  • Experience the beautiful Simien Mountains

    The Simien Mountains National Park is a breath taking landscape of high mountain peaks and plateaus. The area is a walkers' paradise, with interesting trekking to suit varying levels of ability. You will be sure to enjoy close contact with troops of Gelada Baboons, see the giant Lammergeier soaring overhead and observe a wide range of indigenous flora and fauna. Experience the challenge of trekking to the summit of Ras Dashen, Africa's fourth highest peak (4550m), with stunning views.



  • Learn from a local

    Gismu Syum is a qualified local guide with expert knowledge. He will lead you on an unforgettable journey through his majestic homeland and share its secrets with you.
